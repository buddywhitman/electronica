Sure, here is a list of subtopics that you can cover under AC-DC Converters, DC-DC Converters and Resonant Converters:

1. AC-DC Converters
    * Rectifiers
    * Uncontrolled rectifiers
    * Half-wave rectifiers
    * Full-wave rectifiers
    * Controlled rectifiers
    * Half-controlled rectifiers
    * Full-controlled rectifiers
    * Bridge rectifiers
    * Three-phase rectifiers
2. DC-DC Converters
    * Buck converter
    * Boost converter
    * Buck-boost converter
    * Cuk converter
    * SEPIC converter
    * Zeta converter
3. Resonant Converters
    * Series resonant converters (SRC)
    * Parallel resonant converters (PRC)
    * Series-parallel resonant converters (SPRC)
